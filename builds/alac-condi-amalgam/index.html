Rotation

Granting Alacrity

You give Alacrity with Morph Skills:

  1. Offensive Protocol: Obliterate
  2. Offensive Protocol: Pierce
  3. Defensive Protocol: Thorns
  • You may replace Offensive Protocol: Pierce with another protocol of choice for additional utility.
  • You should use these skills on cooldown to ensure Alacrity doesn't drop.
  • Offensive Protocol: Obliterate and Defensive Protocol: Thorns are your main damage buffs.

DPS Rotation

  1. Precast Flame Turret and DO NOT Detonate Flame Turret
  2. Use all your skills off-cooldown except your non-alac CC and healing skills

CC skills

  1. Static Shock (AED Toolbelt)
  2. Roiling Skies (Spear 4)
  3. Offensive Protocol: Pierce (used in DPS rotation)
  4. Flux State (used in DPS rotation)

Sustainability

  1. Liquid State is an evade stance
  2. A.E.D. can bypass many mechanics.
  3. Stainless Steel is useful for additional heals
  4. Innervating Alloy can be used for condi cleanses
  5.  Relic of the Biomancer or Mist Stranger for Open-world
