The purpose of writing this guide is to provide a competent DPS options with good alacrity uptime without forced movement or weapon swaps. The base attack is quite high and fumbling with your skills is not severely punishing.

The alternative weapon sets (rifle and dagger) are not covered in this guide. The concept for the other weapons is relatively simple and you may find more information on the build guides of other sites such as SnowCrows. This build has intermediate difficulty and although the APM is relatively high (70), most of the button presses are attributed to a single skill, Weakening Whirl (Staff 2), which simplifies the rotation a lot. The rotation can be simplified (reduction of ~ 20 APM) by holding down Staff 2 for continuous casts , while other skills only require around 30 APM.

This build heavily relies on Skritt Scuffle for alacrity. Moving out of the scuffle radius (360) will result in missing alacrity for a significant amount of time.

Rotation and Utilities

  1. Use Stone Summit Cannon and its backfire off-cooldown
  2. Use Skritt Swipe and Skritt Scuffle off-cooldown
    • use your stolen skills charges except for Metal Legion Guitar for alacrity
    • try to catch different stolen skills, you may prioritise skills that provide power/strike damage/initiative
    • use Metal Legion Guitar with Backstab if weapon swap is desired (optional, see SC guide)
  3. Use all your initiative on Weakening Whirl (Staff 2)
  4. Use Canach-Coin Toss if you have nothing better to do or replace it with another utility, do not backfire this.
  • Use Skritt Scuffle when Skritt Swipe is recharging
  • Avoid Skritt Swipe when Skritt Scuffle is active

CC options

  • Metal Legion Guitar
  • Hook Strike (stealth/guitar)
  • Helmet Breaker
  • Hook strike is a powerful CC skill that does not require initiative to cast. This is especially efficient when Metal Legion Guitar is used, giving three charges without the need for stealth.

Sustainability for Solo/OW

This build has a lot of sustainability through its baseline traits Magpie's Defense and Enterprising Aristocrat.

You may equip the following for extra:

  •  Relic of Zakiros
  • Emergency Jade Shield
  • Invigorating Precision

Benchmark

The benchmark can vary greatly depending on which stolen skills you get. The numbers are not grinded for and it is likely you will achieve higher DPS than our attempts.
