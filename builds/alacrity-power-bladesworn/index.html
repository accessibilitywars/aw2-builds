Crowd Control

This build has weak crowd control. Replace Signet of Might with a physical skills that has CC:

  • Bull's Charge
  • Kick

Physical skills provide a short term buff through Peak Performance, so you can mitigate the damage loss for bringing a CC if you use Dragon Trigger right after using one of the above CC skills.
