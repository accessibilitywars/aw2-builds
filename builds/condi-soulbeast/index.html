Barbed Arrows [cDPS]

The purpose of writing this guide is to provide a no-weapon swap method to play a ranged PVE soulbeast while easily hitting the 70% benchmark with minimal effort. This build is low cognition, has few repetitions but has a relatively moderate APM. The skills do not require specific timing and having extra hard CC in the build allows flexible upkeep of Twice as Vicious, which has a rather tight timing on other builds. This is inspired by the Guild Wars 1 skill Barbed Arrows.

The juvenile bee does decent condition damage with a very decent extra 300 points of CC.

Rotation and Utilities

DPS Rotation

Stay merged at all times.

  1. Early Precast - Spike Trap and stack dodges.
  2. Pre-battle - One Wolf Pack + Sharpening Stone + Vulture Stance + Sharpen Spines (if using Bristleback)
  3. General Rotation - Use all non-healing skills on cooldown except F1

Crowd Control

  1. You have decent CC by just using Concussion Shot (Shortbow 4)
  2. You have many other CC skills littered in your weapon/pet/utility kit including the ones used to upkeep Twice as Vicious (see below).
  3. You can precast Sharpening Stone for additional CC just before the CC phase of an encounter.

Sustainability

  1. You may opt to take Healing Spring for better condi cleanse
  2. Quick Shot (Shortbow 3) is an evade that does a step back. This is useful for bosses with pull mechanics.
  3. If you need an extra heal, you may swap your pet to the Wallow for Spiritual Reprieve

Optimisation

Twice as Vicious

  1. Spread out Sharpening Stone, Primal Cry and Concussion Shot to maximise Twice as Vicious uptime.
  2. Use your other pet CC skills if your coverage is falling such as Bumble.

Dodging

  1. Where dodging is necessary, you may delay Quick Shot after the dodge to have better uptime of Light on your Feet.
  2. Bumble can also refresh Light on your Feet.

Benchmark

The build is benched with ascended gear and infusions, skipping Quick Shot (Shortbow 3) to avoid forced movement.
