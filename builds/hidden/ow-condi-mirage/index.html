Staphylomirage [cDPS]

Challenging open world mirage that uses only staff.. but two of them. And weapon swapping.

berdandy August 19, 2024 [ group ] #condi #mesmer #mirage #pof #lowcog #lowphys #lowrep
This build requires: PATH OF FIRE
mesmer (Series)

Note: this build was created for the older 2024-08 balance patch.

Classic dual-staff mirage. This build can be very survivable once you get the hang of it, as you spend 90% of your time evading. Named after staphylococcus, the real world bacteria responsible for 'staph infections'
