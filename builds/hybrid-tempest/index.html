Notes

Press everything off cooldown while in Fire Attunement.

With the addition of Major Grandmaster trait Inferno, burning you do now scales off of your Power instead of Condition Damage. This build attempts to maximise this trait by staying in Fire Attunement as much as possible.

This build does ~55% of your total damage in Burning, with the remaining ~45% in Power, easily making it a Hybrid DPS build.

Rotation

Your rotation consists of a simple opener and a Fire Attunement priority.

Opener:

  1. Always be in Fire Attunement
  2. Dragon's Tooth (Scepter Fire 2)
  3. Firestorm
  4. Flamewall (Focus Fire 4)
  5. Phoenix (Scepter Fire 3)
  6. Fire Shield (Focus Fire 5)
  7. Overload Fire

Fire Attunement Priority:

  1. Overload Fire
  2. Phoenix (Scepter Fire 3)
  3. Flamewall (Focus Fire 4)
  4. Dragon's Tooth (Scepter Fire 2)
  5. Firestorm ( Glyph of Storms in Fire Attunement)
  6. Signet of Fire
  7. Fire Shield (Focus Fire 5)
  8. Glyph of Elementals

This build relies heavily on landing Flamewall (Focus Fire 4) to maintain Â Relic of Fireworks uptime. Each tick of damage from the fire field generated by Flamewall (Focus Fire 4) resets Â Relic of Fireworks duration, allowing you to easily maintain 100% updtime with just 1 skill and no cooldown management.

You should also:

Cast Off Cooldown:

  1. "Feel the Burn!"
  2. Flame Barrage

All of these skills have no cast time and can be cast anytime, even during Overload Fire.

Crowd Control

Your CC skills are:

CC Skills:

  1. Shatterstone (Scepter Water 2)
  2. Freezing Gust (Focus Water 4)
  3. Comet (Focus Water 5)
  4. Blinding Flash (Scepter Air 3)
  5. Gale (Focus Air 5)
  6. Dust Devil (Scepter Earth 3)
  7. Magnetic Wave (Focus Earth 4)

To CC, perform the following:

Simplified CC Rotation:

  1. Water Attunement 2 4 5
  2. Air Attunement 3 5
  3. Earth Attunement 3 4

Then return to Fire Attunement as soon as you are able to.
