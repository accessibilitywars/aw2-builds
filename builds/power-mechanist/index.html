Power Mechanist

Warning: this build was created for an earlier balance patch. It may not perform up to expectations.

Signet-heavy, with some chill hammer thumping. And a little turret on the side.

Gearing

  • Full Berserker's
  • Runes of the Scholar
  • Hammer, Sigils of Force & Impact
  • Relic of the Thief
  • Consumables:
    • Cilantro Lime Sous-Vide Steak (Power/Ferocity)
    • Superior Sharpening Stone (Power)

Notes

To play this build, you want to place

  • Rifle Turret

at the start of the fight, then press

  1. Thunderclap (Hammer 5)
  2. Electro-whirl (Hammer 2)
  3. Overclock Signet

off cooldown, and that is basically your rotation.

Crowd Control

You have decent CC. Primarily, Rocket Charge (Hammer 3) will make your Mech do Rocket Punch, which is a CC, but do take note that since the cooldown of Rocket Punch is longer than that of Rocket Charge it will not always be available.

Other CC skills that you have are:

  • Force Signet
  • Overclock Signet

If you still need more CC, replace

  • Rifle Turret ERROR

with

  • Throw Mine
