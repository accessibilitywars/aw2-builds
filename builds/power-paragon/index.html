Notes

This raid build focuses on utilizing Warrior's strong Dual Wielding trait on Paragon, allowing for a selfless low intensity DPS that's great for group content.

It requires the Weaponmaster Training from Secrets of the Obscure to be able to wield the dagger as Paragon.

It also has high burst APM, as there is a 5 button combo that you ideally press quickly. Expect lower DPS if you do not press this combo quickly.

Consider it a more selfless alternative to the Magebane Daggerbreaker build.

Rotation

At the start of the fight, use all of the signets you have, saving Signet of Fury for right when you are about to attack the boss.

  1. Breaching Strike (Dagger F1)
  2. Signet of Fury (won't be available in opener)
  3. Chant of Action (F2) (only if it is not active or your motivation is less than 7)
  4. Crushing Blow (Mace 4)
  5. Tremor (Mace 5)
  6. Crushing Blow (Mace 4)
  7. Auto until F1 is up again
  8. Breaching Strike (Dagger F1)
  9. Disrupting Stab (Dagger 3)
  10. Auto until Mace 5 has less than 2 seconds left on its cooldown

Notes

Relic

Upkeep Relic of the Claw with Tremor (Mace 5) and Disrupting Stab (Dagger 3). The rotation is made in such a way that you are reaching ~90% uptime of Claw without much thought. For slightly higher Claw uptime, delay your Dagger 3 a little bit instead of doing it immediately after your Dagger F1.

You can also use Relic of the Eagle for a DPS loss of ~3k.

Weapon Set 2 options

Dagger / Axe for more DPS but higher APM

Your rotation becomes:

  1. Breaching Strike (Dagger F1)
  2. Signet of Fury (won't be available in opener)
  3. Chant of Action (F2) (if it is not active or your motivation is less than 7)
  4. Crushing Blow (Mace 4)
  5. Tremor (Mace 5)
  6. Crushing Blow (Mace 4)
  7. Weapon swap
  8. Dual Strike (Axe 4)
  9. Whirling Axe (Axe 5)
  10. Auto until F1 is up again
  11. Breaching Strike (Dagger F1)
  12. Disrupting Stab (Dagger 3)
  13. Weapon swap
  14. Auto until Mace 5 has 2 seconds left on its cooldown

Chants

Your F2's refrain is what you will be camping most of the time. However, don't forget your other chants.

Most notably, Chant of Freedom is a group stun break and also gives stability. To use it, simply press it, then immediately use your F2 again; it's a 15% DPS loss to forget to head back to your F2 chant, so make sure to do so.

Utilities and Elites

Signet of Fury is the only utility skill truly required for this build, as removing all of the other Signets results in only a 3k DPS loss. When the build already is getting ~35k, this is more than acceptable if you're willing to bring utility for your team. Discuss with your allies if there's anything that needs covered, such as CC, Resistance, Stability, etc.

As far as what to replace, always keep your Signet of Fury as it's a big DPS loss to remove it. Removing Signet of Might is also a DPS loss, but if your team needs extra help it's still worth taking off.

Elites

For your elites, you have two options:

Would you like to resurrect downed people, up to five, almost instantly? Battle Standard is an easy choice and is the usual default; it's hard to wrong here.

Would you like to prevent mistakes from ever happening to begin with, or ignore mechanics that would otherwise down the whole group? Feel confident in timing it correctly? "We Will Never Yield!" is your new best friend. Some places you can bring it include:

  • Sunqua Peak CM (for all of the spread mechanics)
  • Conjured Amalgamate (for the clap)
  • Cardinal Sabir (ignoring tornadoes / "uppies" and "corners")
  • Deimos CM (for a guaranteed chill Mind Crush in case Aegis gets stripped, such as if oils were spread at the same time as Mind Crush)

Utilities

Consider for your utilities:

  • Throw Bolas for long-range immobilization.
  • Kick, Bull's Charge, "On Your Knees!" and Banner of Strength for more CC.
  • Banner of Tactics for superspeed, stability, resistance and group stun break.
  • "Never Surrender!" for resistance, aegis, resolution, and group stun break.
  • Banner of Defense for barrier or aegis.
  • "Brace Yourselves!" for barrier.
  • Endure Pain or Defiant Stance for ignoring damage, allowing you to skip mechanics like Deimos's Mind Crush ("Stand in the ward!").
