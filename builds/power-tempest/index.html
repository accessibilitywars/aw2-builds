Notes

Simple, step based rotation with most of your damage in Air Attunement.

The concept of this build is to stay in Air Attunement and Overload Air as much as possible, while swapping to Fire Attunement both to fill in some of the down time and to reset Overload Air cooldown.

Rotation

Your rotation consists of a precasted opener, a Fire Attunement loop, and an Air Attunement priority:

Precasted Opener:

  1. Start in Fire Attunement
  2. Glyph of Elementals
  3. Air Attunement
  4. Overload Air

Fire Attunement Loop:

  1. Cauterizing Strike (Sword Fire 3)
  2. Ring of Fire (Dagger Fire 4)
  3. Fire Grab (Dagger Fire 5)
  4. Flame Uprising (Sword Fire 2)
  5. Air Attunement during the cast of Flame Uprising (Sword Fire 2)

Skip any skills that are on cooldown, primarily Fire Grab (Dagger Fire 5), which will be on cooldown every other loop.

Air Attunement Priority:

  1. Overload Air
  2. Quantum Strike (Sword Air 3)
  3. Ride the Lightning (Dagger Air 4)
  4. Fire Attunement as soon as it is off cooldown

Try to always start with your Precasted Opener, before entering your main attunement looping below. As much as possible you want to hit your precast Overload Air, but that is not always possible on all bosses. If you do not have enough time for your full precast, start in Air Attunement, precast Overload Air as you are running towards the boss, and continue the rotation from there, casting Glyph of Elementals in your first Fire Attunement loop.

Following the above rotation will allow you to maintain 100% uptime on Â Relic of Fireworks without any additional management.

You should also:

Cast Off Cooldown:

  1. "Feel the Burn!"
  2. Flame Barrage

All of these skills have no cast time and can be cast anytime, even during Overload Air.

 

Crowd Control

Your CC skills are:

  1. Polaric Leap (Sword Air 2)
  2. Updraft (Dagger Air 5)
  3. Earthquake (Dagger Earth 4)
  4. Churning Earth (Dagger Earth 5)

Polaric Leap (Sword Air 2) and Updraft (Dagger Air 5) will be on your bar whenever you are in Air Attunement.

If more CC is required, instead of your Fire Attunement Loop, you can instead perform:

Earth Attunement Loop:

  1. Earthquake (Dagger Earth 4)
  2. Churning Earth (Dagger Earth 5)
  3. Air Attunement during the cast of Churning Earth (Dagger Earth 5)

Immediately after Overload Air.
